Public Access & Unique IP Links

How to Share Unique Links

You can now generate Unique Links for every product!

1. Go to the main website home page.
2. Click the Share Icon on any product card.
3. The unique link (e.g., #product-ram-001) is copied to your clipboard.
4. Send this link to anyone on your network!

How to Get a PUBLIC IP Address

To share your website with people outside your WiFi DO NOT USE IPCONFIG, you need a tunneling service like ngrok.

1. Download ngrok from ngrok.com 2. Open Command Prompt and run: ngrok http 8080 3. Copy the 'Forwarding' URL (e.g., https://a1b2c3d4.ngrok.io)

Note: The generated ngrok URL is your "Unique Public IP Link".
